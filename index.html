DealPal

A tiny friendly Chrome extension that scans product pages, tracks the latest price, and helps you save money.

How DealPal works

  • Visit any product page on Amazon, eBay or Walmart
  • Click the DealPal icon in your Chrome toolbar
  • Click Scan page for product to save the current price
  • Click the Shop on Amazon button to buy and support DealPal at no extra cost

Privacy note: DealPal works locally by default. The Share anonymized price changes option is optional and turned off by default.
